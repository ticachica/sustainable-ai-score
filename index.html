      Two-tier reality

      Local + solar (28/30) far outranks any cloud provider (8–20/30). The single biggest sustainability lever is running inference locally.

      Training transparency gap

      Even the best providers (Meta at 4/5) don't publish full lifecycle carbon assessments. Most score 1–2/5.

      Efficiency ≠ sustainability

      DeepSeek is the most efficient cloud architecture but runs on ~60% coal grid. Architecture alone isn't enough — grid matters.

      xAI is an outlier

      At 8/30 with a 5-point gap to the next worst, xAI is in a category of its own. Active Memphis lawsuit, zero transparency.
